WHAT WE BELEIVE

CrossPoint Baptist Church belongs to the Baptist Union of
Victoria, along with the BUV and other churches of the
BUV we believe:

  • The Bible is God’s written Word to mankind. Delivered to mankind through divine and holy inspiration. It is the source of supreme authority for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness.
  • God is one God in three persons – the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit.
  • That God is Holy and He created man for an eternal and personal relationship for Himself.
